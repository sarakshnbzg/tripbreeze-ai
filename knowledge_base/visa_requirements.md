# Visa and Entry Requirements by Destination

## France (Schengen Area)
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days within 180 days. ETIAS authorization required from 2025.
- **EU/EEA citizens:** No visa required. Freedom of movement.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days within 180 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Schengen visa required. Apply at French consulate. Processing: 15 working days.
- **Chinese citizens:** Schengen visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 3+ months beyond stay, return ticket, proof of accommodation, travel insurance (€30,000 minimum coverage).

## Japan
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days for tourism.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply at Japanese embassy. Processing: 5–7 working days.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid throughout stay, return ticket, proof of funds.
- **Special note:** Visit Japan Web registration recommended for smooth customs/immigration.

## United States
- **EU citizens:** ESTA required (visa waiver). Apply online, valid 2 years. Cost: $21.
- **UK citizens:** ESTA required.
- **Australian citizens:** ESTA required.
- **Indian citizens:** B1/B2 visa required. Interview at US embassy. Processing varies widely.
- **Chinese citizens:** B1/B2 visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid for duration of stay, ESTA or visa, return ticket.

## Thailand
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days (extendable by 30 days at immigration).
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa on arrival for 15 days, or e-visa for 60 days.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days (as of 2024 policy).
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, proof of onward travel, proof of accommodation.

## Spain (Schengen Area)
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days within 180 days. ETIAS required from 2025.
- **EU/EEA citizens:** No visa required.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days within 180 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Schengen visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Same as France (Schengen rules apply).

## UAE (Dubai)
- **US citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days (free).
- **EU citizens:** Visa on arrival for 90 days (free).
- **UK citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days (free).
- **Indian citizens:** e-Visa required (apply online, 96-hour or 30-day options).
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days (free).
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket, proof of accommodation.

## Italy (Schengen Area)
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days within 180 days. ETIAS required from 2025.
- **EU/EEA citizens:** No visa required.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days within 180 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Schengen visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Same Schengen requirements as France.

## Australia
- **US citizens:** ETA (Electronic Travel Authority) required. Apply via app. Cost: AUD 20.
- **EU citizens:** ETA or eVisitor visa (free for most EU countries).
- **UK citizens:** eVisitor visa (free, valid up to 3 months).
- **Indian citizens:** Visitor visa required. Apply online. Processing: 20–30 days.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visitor visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, proof of funds, return ticket, health insurance recommended.

## Turkey
- **US citizens:** e-Visa required. Apply online. Cost: $50. Valid 90 days within 180 days.
- **EU citizens:** Varies by country. Many visa-free for 90 days.
- **UK citizens:** e-Visa required. Cost: varies.
- **Indian citizens:** e-Visa required.
- **Chinese citizens:** e-Visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket.

## Indonesia (Bali)
- **US citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35 (extendable once for 30 days).
- **EU citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35.
- **UK citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa-free for 30 days (as of recent policy).
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket, proof of accommodation.

## General Travel Tips
- Always check the latest visa requirements before travel — policies change frequently.
- Keep digital and physical copies of all travel documents.
- Register with your country's embassy when traveling to high-risk areas.
- Travel insurance is strongly recommended for all international trips.
- Some countries require proof of yellow fever vaccination if arriving from endemic areas.
