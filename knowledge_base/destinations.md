# Popular Destination Guides

## Paris, France
- **Best time to visit:** April–June and September–October for mild weather and fewer crowds.
- **Top attractions:** Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Montmartre, Champs-Élysées.
- **Local transport:** Paris Métro is efficient and affordable. Buy a carnet of 10 tickets or a Navigo weekly pass.
- **Budget tip:** Many museums are free on the first Sunday of each month. Picnicking along the Seine saves on dining costs.
- **Currency:** Euro (EUR). Credit cards widely accepted.
- **Language:** French. English is spoken in tourist areas.
- **Safety:** Generally safe. Watch for pickpockets at tourist hotspots and on the Métro.
- **Average daily budget:** €100–€200 mid-range; €50–€80 budget.

## Tokyo, Japan
- **Best time to visit:** March–May (cherry blossom season) and September–November (autumn foliage).
- **Top attractions:** Shibuya Crossing, Senso-ji Temple, Meiji Shrine, Tsukiji Outer Market, Akihabara.
- **Local transport:** JR Pass for tourists covers most trains. Suica/Pasmo IC cards for subway and buses.
- **Budget tip:** Conveyor belt sushi and ramen shops offer excellent meals for ¥500–¥1000. 100-yen shops for souvenirs.
- **Currency:** Japanese Yen (JPY). Cash is still king in many places; carry yen.
- **Language:** Japanese. English signage in major stations; limited English elsewhere.
- **Safety:** One of the safest major cities in the world. Very low crime rate.
- **Average daily budget:** ¥10,000–¥20,000 mid-range; ¥5,000–¥8,000 budget.

## New York City, USA
- **Best time to visit:** April–June and September–November for pleasant weather.
- **Top attractions:** Statue of Liberty, Central Park, Times Square, Brooklyn Bridge, Metropolitan Museum of Art.
- **Local transport:** NYC Subway runs 24/7. Get an OMNY contactless pass or MetroCard.
- **Budget tip:** Free Staten Island Ferry for Statue of Liberty views. Many museums have pay-what-you-wish hours.
- **Currency:** US Dollar (USD).
- **Language:** English. Highly multilingual city.
- **Safety:** Generally safe in tourist areas. Standard big-city precautions apply.
- **Average daily budget:** $150–$300 mid-range; $80–$120 budget.

## Bangkok, Thailand
- **Best time to visit:** November–February (cool and dry season).
- **Top attractions:** Grand Palace, Wat Pho, Chatuchak Weekend Market, Khao San Road, Chao Phraya River.
- **Local transport:** BTS Skytrain and MRT subway. Tuk-tuks and river boats for short trips.
- **Budget tip:** Street food is excellent and cheap (฿30–฿60 per dish). Negotiate prices at markets.
- **Currency:** Thai Baht (THB). ATMs widely available; credit cards accepted at malls.
- **Language:** Thai. English spoken in tourist areas and hotels.
- **Safety:** Generally safe. Be cautious of common tourist scams (gem shops, inflated tuk-tuk prices).
- **Average daily budget:** ฿1,500–฿3,000 mid-range; ฿800–฿1,200 budget.

## Barcelona, Spain
- **Best time to visit:** May–June and September–October for warm weather without extreme heat.
- **Top attractions:** Sagrada Familia, Park Güell, La Rambla, Gothic Quarter, Camp Nou.
- **Local transport:** TMB Metro is efficient. T-Casual card for 10 trips.
- **Budget tip:** Free walking tours available. Beach access is free. Lunch menú del día offers 3-course meals for €10–€15.
- **Currency:** Euro (EUR).
- **Language:** Catalan and Spanish. English widely understood in tourist areas.
- **Safety:** Watch for pickpockets on La Rambla and in crowded areas.
- **Average daily budget:** €80–€150 mid-range; €40–€70 budget.

## Dubai, UAE
- **Best time to visit:** November–March for cooler temperatures (20–30°C).
- **Top attractions:** Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Marina, Gold Souk.
- **Local transport:** Dubai Metro is modern and affordable. Nol card for all public transport.
- **Budget tip:** Visit during shoulder season for lower hotel rates. Free beaches at JBR.
- **Currency:** UAE Dirham (AED). Credit cards widely accepted.
- **Language:** Arabic. English is widely spoken as a business language.
- **Safety:** Extremely safe. Very low crime rate. Strict laws — respect local customs.
- **Average daily budget:** AED 500–1,000 mid-range; AED 250–400 budget.

## Rome, Italy
- **Best time to visit:** April–May and September–October.
- **Top attractions:** Colosseum, Vatican Museums, Pantheon, Trevi Fountain, Roman Forum.
- **Local transport:** Metro lines A and B. Bus network covers the rest. Roma Pass for transport + museum entry.
- **Budget tip:** Free entry to Pantheon and many churches. Drink from public water fountains (nasoni).
- **Currency:** Euro (EUR).
- **Language:** Italian. English in tourist areas.
- **Safety:** Pickpocketing common near tourist sites and on buses. Otherwise safe.
- **Average daily budget:** €80–€160 mid-range; €40–€70 budget.

## Sydney, Australia
- **Best time to visit:** September–November and March–May for mild weather.
- **Top attractions:** Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge, Bondi Beach, Taronga Zoo, The Rocks.
- **Local transport:** Opal card for trains, buses, ferries, and light rail.
- **Budget tip:** Many coastal walks are free (Bondi to Coogee). BYO restaurants save on dining.
- **Currency:** Australian Dollar (AUD).
- **Language:** English.
- **Safety:** Very safe. Sun protection is essential — UV index is high.
- **Average daily budget:** AUD 150–300 mid-range; AUD 80–120 budget.

## Istanbul, Turkey
- **Best time to visit:** April–May and September–November.
- **Top attractions:** Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Grand Bazaar, Topkapi Palace, Bosphorus Cruise.
- **Local transport:** Istanbulkart for metro, tram, bus, and ferries.
- **Budget tip:** Street food (simit, balık ekmek) is delicious and cheap. Haggle at the Grand Bazaar.
- **Currency:** Turkish Lira (TRY). USD and EUR sometimes accepted.
- **Language:** Turkish. English in tourist areas.
- **Safety:** Generally safe for tourists. Standard precautions in crowded areas.
- **Average daily budget:** ₺800–₺1,500 mid-range; ₺400–₺700 budget.

## Bali, Indonesia
- **Best time to visit:** April–October (dry season).
- **Top attractions:** Ubud Rice Terraces, Tanah Lot Temple, Uluwatu Temple, Seminyak Beach, Mount Batur.
- **Local transport:** Scooter rental is popular but risky. Grab (ride-hailing) is convenient.
- **Budget tip:** Warungs (local eateries) serve meals for Rp 20,000–40,000. Negotiate for tours.
- **Currency:** Indonesian Rupiah (IDR).
- **Language:** Indonesian (Bahasa). English widely spoken in tourist areas.
- **Safety:** Generally safe. Watch for traffic and strong ocean currents.
- **Average daily budget:** Rp 500,000–1,500,000 mid-range; Rp 250,000–500,000 budget.
