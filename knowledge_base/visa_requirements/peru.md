## Peru
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 183 days.
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 183 days.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days (extendable).
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 183 days.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 183 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply at Peruvian consulate.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required (unless holding valid US/Canada/UK/Schengen/Australia visa — then visa-free).
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket.
