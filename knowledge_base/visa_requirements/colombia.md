## Colombia
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days (extendable to 180 days).
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply at Colombian consulate.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket, proof of funds.
- **Special note:** Check-Mig form must be completed online 24 hours before arrival.
