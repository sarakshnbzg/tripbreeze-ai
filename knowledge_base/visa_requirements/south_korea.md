## South Korea
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days. K-ETA required (apply online, valid 2 years, KRW 10,000).
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 6 months. K-ETA required.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days. K-ETA required (waived for some EU countries).
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days. K-ETA required.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days. K-ETA required.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply at Korean embassy. Processing: 5–7 working days.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required.
- **Japanese citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket, K-ETA approval.
