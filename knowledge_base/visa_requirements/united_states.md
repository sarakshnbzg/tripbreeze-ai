## United States
- **EU citizens (VWP countries):** ESTA required (Visa Waiver Program). Apply online, valid 2 years. Cost: $21.
- **UK citizens:** ESTA required. Same conditions.
- **Australian citizens:** ESTA required.
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free. No ESTA needed. Just valid passport.
- **Japanese citizens:** ESTA required.
- **South Korean citizens:** ESTA required.
- **Indian citizens:** B1/B2 visa required. Interview at US embassy. Processing varies widely (weeks to months). Cost: $185.
- **Chinese citizens:** B1/B2 visa required. Interview required. Cost: $185.
- **Brazilian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days (as of 2024 agreement).
- **Colombian citizens:** B1/B2 visa required.
- **South African citizens:** B1/B2 visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid for duration of stay (6 months recommended), ESTA or visa, return ticket.
