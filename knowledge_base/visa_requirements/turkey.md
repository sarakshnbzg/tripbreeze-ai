## Turkey
- **US citizens:** e-Visa required. Apply online. Cost: $50. Valid 90 days within 180 days, multiple entry.
- **Canadian citizens:** e-Visa required. Cost: $60 CAD.
- **EU citizens:** Varies by country. Many visa-free for 90 days (Germany, France, Netherlands: visa-free. Spain, Italy: visa-free).
- **UK citizens:** e-Visa required. Cost: varies.
- **Australian citizens:** e-Visa required. Cost: $60 AUD.
- **Indian citizens:** e-Visa required.
- **Chinese citizens:** e-Visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket.
