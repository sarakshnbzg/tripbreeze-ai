## South Africa
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days (most EU countries).
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply at South African embassy. Processing: 5–10 working days.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 30+ days beyond departure, 2 blank pages, return ticket, proof of funds, yellow fever certificate if arriving from endemic area.
