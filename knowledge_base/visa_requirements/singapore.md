## Singapore
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days (most EU countries).
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply online via Singapore Immigration (ICA). Processing: 3–5 working days. Cost: SGD 30.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required. Must be sponsored by a Singapore-based entity or travel agency.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, proof of onward travel, proof of accommodation, proof of funds.
- **Special note:** Singapore Arrival Card (SGAC) must be submitted online within 3 days before arrival (free).
