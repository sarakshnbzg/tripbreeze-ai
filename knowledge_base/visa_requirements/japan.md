## Japan
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days for tourism.
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply at Japanese embassy. Processing: 5–7 working days. Cost: varies.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required. Single/multiple entry options.
- **Brazilian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **South African citizens:** Visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid throughout stay, return ticket, proof of funds.
- **Special note:** Visit Japan Web registration recommended for smooth customs/immigration.
