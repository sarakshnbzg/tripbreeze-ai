## Thailand
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days (extendable by 30 days at immigration office for THB 1,900).
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa on arrival for 15 days (THB 2,000), or e-visa for 60 days.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days (as of 2024 policy).
- **South African citizens:** Visa-free for 30 days.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, proof of onward travel, proof of accommodation, proof of funds (THB 20,000 in cash may be checked).
