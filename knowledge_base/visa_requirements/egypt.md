## Egypt
- **US citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $25. Or e-visa online.
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $25.
- **EU citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $25.
- **UK citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $25. Or e-visa.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $25.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply at Egyptian embassy. Or e-visa available.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required. Apply at embassy.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, 2 blank pages, return ticket.
