## Indonesia (Bali)
- **US citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35 (extendable once for 30 days at immigration for $35).
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35.
- **EU citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35.
- **UK citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa on arrival for 30 days. Cost: $35.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa-free for 30 days (as of recent policy).
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket, proof of accommodation.
