## Nepal
- **US citizens:** Visa on arrival at Kathmandu airport. Cost: $30 (15 days), $50 (30 days), $125 (90 days). Also available as e-visa online.
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa on arrival. Same fees.
- **EU citizens:** Visa on arrival. Same fees.
- **UK citizens:** Visa on arrival. Same fees.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa on arrival. Same fees.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days (as of recent policy).
- **Indian citizens:** Visa-free. No visa or passport needed — valid ID sufficient due to open border treaty.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, 2 passport photos, cash in USD for visa fee.
- **Special note:** Trekking permits (TIMS card + conservation area permits) are separate from tourist visa. Arrange in Kathmandu or Pokhara.
