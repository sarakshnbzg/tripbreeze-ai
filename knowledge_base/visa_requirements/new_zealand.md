## New Zealand
- **US citizens:** NZeTA required. Apply via app or online. Cost: NZD 17–23. Valid 2 years. International Visitor Conservation and Tourism Levy (IVL): NZD 35.
- **Canadian citizens:** NZeTA required. Same conditions.
- **UK citizens:** NZeTA required.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free. No NZeTA needed.
- **EU citizens:** NZeTA required for most.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa required. Apply online. Processing: 20–25 working days.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa required. Apply online or at visa center.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 3+ months beyond departure, return ticket, proof of funds (NZD 1,000/month or NZD 400/month with prepaid accommodation).
