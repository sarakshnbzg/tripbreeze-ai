## Vietnam
- **US citizens:** Visa required. E-visa available (apply online). Cost: $25. Valid 30 days, single entry. Or visa on arrival with pre-approved letter.
- **Canadian citizens:** E-visa required. Cost: $25.
- **EU citizens:** Many visa-free for up to 45 days (France, Germany, Italy, Spain, UK). Others need e-visa.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 45 days.
- **Australian citizens:** E-visa required. Cost: $25.
- **Indian citizens:** E-visa required. Cost: $25.
- **Chinese citizens:** Visa-free for up to 30 days.
- **Japanese citizens:** Visa-free for up to 45 days.
- **South Korean citizens:** Visa-free for up to 45 days.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months, 2 blank pages, return ticket.
