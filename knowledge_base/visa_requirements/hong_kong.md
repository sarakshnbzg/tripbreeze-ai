## Hong Kong (China SAR)
- **US citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Canadian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **EU citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **UK citizens:** Visa-free for up to 180 days.
- **Australian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Indian citizens:** Visa-free for up to 14 days. Pre-arrival registration online recommended.
- **Chinese citizens (mainland):** Exit-Entry Permit required.
- **Japanese citizens:** Visa-free for up to 90 days.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 1+ month beyond stay, proof of onward travel.
- **Special note:** Hong Kong is separate from mainland China — different visa requirements.
