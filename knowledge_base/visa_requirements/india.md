## India
- **US citizens:** e-Visa required. Apply online. 30-day e-Tourist visa: $25. 1-year/5-year options available. Processing: 72 hours.
- **Canadian citizens:** e-Visa required. Same process.
- **UK citizens:** e-Visa required. Cost: $25 (30-day).
- **Australian citizens:** e-Visa required.
- **EU citizens:** e-Visa required for most countries.
- **Chinese citizens:** e-Visa available but restricted. Apply at embassy for longer stays.
- **Japanese citizens:** e-Visa required.
- **South African citizens:** e-Visa required.
- **Documents needed:** Passport valid 6+ months with 2 blank pages, passport photo (specific requirements), return ticket.
- **Special note:** Different visa categories (e-Tourist, e-Business, e-Medical). Apply at least 4 days before travel.
